# 💡 Insights Summary

This file summarises the key business insights supported by the **Inventory Intelligence Dashboard**.

## Key Insights
- The dashboard provides visibility into how inventory is distributed across plants and materials.
- Inventory composition analysis helps distinguish between **On-Hand**, **Work-in-Progress (WIP)**, and **In-Transit** inventory.
- **Days on Hand (DOH)** helps identify slower-moving inventory and potential working capital lock-up.
- Plant-level comparison highlights where the highest inventory concentration exists.
- Material-level analysis supports identification of high-value or high-attention inventory items.
- Exception-focused views improve visibility into stock positions that may require operational follow-up.

## Business Interpretation
The dashboard is designed to support inventory monitoring and management reporting by helping users:
- understand where inventory value is concentrated
- monitor stock movement and inventory composition
- identify potential risk areas through DOH and stock-related exceptions
- improve decision-making through centralised KPI-based reporting

## Operational Value
This dashboard can help business users:
- improve inventory visibility across operational units
- monitor capital tied up in stock
- track inventory states more effectively
- support timely intervention through exception-based reporting
